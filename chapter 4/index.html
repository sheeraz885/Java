VARIABLE NAMES: LEGAL & ILLEGAL

legal and illegal variable names in JavaScript:

Legal Variable Names:

    1. Starts with a letter, underscore, or dollar sign:

  • myVar
  • _myVar
  • $myVar

    • 2. Can contain letters, digits, underscores, and dollar signs:

  • myVar1
  • my_variable
  • var$Name

    • 3. Case-sensitive:

  • myVar and myvar are different variables.
Illegal Variable Names:

    1.Starts with a digit:

  • 1myVar (illegal)

    • 2.Contains spaces:

  • my Var (illegal)

    • 3.Uses reserved keywords:

  • if, for, function, etc. (illegal)

    • 4.Special characters other than _ and $:

  • my-var (illegal)
